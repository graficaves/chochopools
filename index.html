FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pool services

We recommend weekly pool maintenance to ensure optimal water quality and equipment performance. This includes skimming, vacuuming, chemical balancing, and equipment inspection. Regular service prevents costly repairs and keeps your pool sparkling clean year-round.

Our complete maintenance service includes: surface skimming, vacuuming, brushing walls and tiles, emptying skimmer baskets, chemical testing and balancing (pH, chlorine, alkalinity), equipment inspection, filter cleaning, and detailed monthly reports of your pool's condition.

Yes! We provide emergency repair services for urgent pool issues like equipment failures, major leaks, or water quality emergencies. Contact us at (631) 804-9119 for immediate assistance. Our experienced technicians are ready to diagnose and fix your pool problems quickly.

A typical pool construction project takes 6-12 weeks from start to finish, depending on the complexity, size, and customization. This includes permitting, excavation, installation, finishing, and final inspections. We provide a detailed timeline during your free consultation.
